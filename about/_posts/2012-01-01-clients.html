Ernst & Young

Lunatech Research has been working with Ernst & Young Indirect Tax to implement web services for their customers and internal advisors. Lunatech's role has been to work with the customer to clarify the users requirements, translate this into technical requirements, design the systems and architecture, and finally implement the application.

Sdu Uitgevers

Sdu Uitgevers is the former Dutch Government Printing Office. This company has moved into all forms of electronic publishing, including on- line systems. Since 1995, Lunatech has assisted the Sdu in designing and developing their on-line and back-office systems. These systems include most kinds of official government publications, documents from the houses of parliament, the official publications of all laws and changes to laws, and most other output of the Dutch government.

UPS Supply Chain Solutions

UPS-SCS provides supply chain logistics and management. Lunatech Research has been working with UPS Logistics Group by designing and developing a suite of applications with tracking capabilities, message switching, and middleware that integrates into various legacy systems for their multi-national customers. Lunatech also provides a high level of project management for the projects we are involved in.

Other clients include: Addison Wesley, Apple Computer, AT&T Unisource, Hewlett Packard, Kaplan Inc. Morgan Stanley Bank, NOB / NOS, Sun Microsystems and TNO Automotive
