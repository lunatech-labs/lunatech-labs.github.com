Some of the initiatives we are involved with:

010DEV

In 2012, Lunatech helped launch 010DEV to create a vibrant software development community in the Rotterdam area. 010DEV is a group of developers with a broad interest in software development concepts and aims to create a space where people of different backgrounds can exchange their knowledge and ideas.

Play!ground

In 2011, Lunatech hosted the first Play framework developer event, with both technical and practical presentations about using Play.

More Play!ground Events

Lunatech golf invitational

Since 2006, Lunatech has organised golf a tournament in September, with a variety of invited players. There is a golf clinic for the less experienced and plenty of opportunity to socialise.

More Golf Events

Bootstrapping & Agility

The Bootstrapping and agility meet-up was set-up in 2009 to bring together entrepreneurs, investors, customers and software developers to discuss how to apply bootstrap and agile techniques when starting new ventures, or software projects within established organisations. Events include management-level presentations about bootstrapping software projects. The group meets several times per year.

Rotterdam art exhibitions

Displayed in our office is the work of local artists, although we are also honored with works from further afield. From time to time we have exhibitions and openings, to show new talent and provide a setting that is special for guests, colleagues and partners alike.
