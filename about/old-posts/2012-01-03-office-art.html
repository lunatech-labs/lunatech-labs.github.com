On Sunday June 26th 2005, we started hosting an exhibition of 20 impressions of Vermeers famous Girl with a Pearl Earring. They were exhibited in the Lunatech Research Board Room until November 2005. In May 2006 the Homage to Rembrant exhibition graced the walls - something a little different. If would like to exhibit at the Lunatech offices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
